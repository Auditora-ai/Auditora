# Cookie Policy

**Last updated: April 3, 2026**

This Cookie Policy explains how Auditora.ai ("we", "us", "our") uses cookies and similar tracking technologies when you visit our website at auditora.ai and use our platform. This policy should be read alongside our [Privacy Policy](/legal/privacy-policy).

## 1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device (computer, tablet, or mobile) when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently, provide a better browsing experience, and give website operators information about how their site is being used.

## 2. Types of Cookies We Use

### 2.1 Essential Cookies (Strictly Necessary)

These cookies are required for the basic functionality of our website and platform. They cannot be disabled without affecting the core features of the service.

| Cookie | Purpose | Duration |
|--------|---------|----------|
| `session_token` | Maintains your authenticated session | Session / 30 days |
| `csrf_token` | Protects against cross-site request forgery attacks | Session |
| `locale` | Stores your selected language preference | 1 year |
| `cookie_consent` | Remembers your cookie consent preferences | 1 year |

**Legal basis:** These cookies are necessary for the performance of our contract with you and for our legitimate interests in operating a secure platform.

### 2.2 Analytics Cookies

We use analytics cookies to understand how visitors interact with our website. This helps us improve our platform and provide a better experience.

| Cookie | Purpose | Provider | Duration |
|--------|---------|----------|----------|
| `ph_*` | Product analytics: page views, feature usage, user flows | PostHog | 1 year |
| `_ga` / `_gid` | Website traffic analytics | Google Analytics | 2 years / 24 hours |

**Legal basis:** These cookies are set only with your consent. You can opt out at any time through our cookie settings.

**Note:** PostHog is configured to respect Do Not Track (DNT) headers and Global Privacy Control (GPC) signals. When these signals are detected, analytics cookies are not set.

### 2.3 Preference Cookies

These cookies remember your choices to provide a more personalized experience.

| Cookie | Purpose | Duration |
|--------|---------|----------|
| `theme` | Remembers your preferred color theme (light/dark) | 1 year |
| `locale` | Stores your preferred language (en, es, de, fr) | 1 year |

**Legal basis:** Legitimate interest in providing a personalized user experience.

For more information about these services, please refer to their respective privacy policies.

## 4. How to Manage Cookies

### Cookie Settings on Our Site

You can manage your cookie preferences at any time by clicking the **"Cookie Settings"** button in the footer of our website. This allows you to enable or disable optional cookie categories.

### Browser Settings

Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings. Here's how to manage cookies in popular browsers:

- **Chrome**: Settings → Privacy and security → Cookies and other site data
- **Firefox**: Settings → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data
- **Safari**: Preferences → Privacy → Manage Website Data
- **Edge**: Settings → Cookies and site permissions → Cookies and site data

### Do Not Track / Global Privacy Control

We respect **Do Not Track (DNT)** and **Global Privacy Control (GPC)** signals. When these signals are detected in your browser, we automatically disable analytics and marketing cookies.

## 5. Cookie Consent

When you first visit our website, you will see a cookie consent banner that allows you to:

- **Accept all cookies** — enables all cookie categories
- **Reject non-essential cookies** — only essential cookies will be set
- **Customize** — choose which cookie categories to enable

You can change your preferences at any time via the Cookie Settings button in our footer.
